The **Culinary Generator** burns food items to produce FE. The better the food, the more FE it produces.

The total FE produced is `nutrition * saturation * 8000`.
The FE per tick is `nutrition * 8`.

If the total FE produced is greater than 64,000, the remaining FE is drastically lowered. The same is true if the FE per tick is greater than 64.

## Configuration

The Culinary Generator can currently not be configured.
